About Me

My name is Quanxiang Liu (刘权祥). I am currently a Ph.D. student in Flight Mechanics and Control at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Prof. Bing Wang. Before that, I received my master’s degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University, supervised by Prof. Yuchao Dai, and my bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from the same university. My research interests lie in spatial intelligence and robotics.
